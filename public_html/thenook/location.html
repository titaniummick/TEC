Location & Map

Centrally located THE Nook is easily accessible from all means of transportation, from Bangkok Suvarnabhumi international airport via taxi or the airport link and Bangkok Don Muang domestic airport via taxi through the express way.

Located in the heart of Pratunam THE NooK is near numerous facilities available right at your doorstep, most areas of interest (please go to our links) are within walking distant and both taxis and “Tuk Tuks” (Thailand three wheel taxi) are available if you wish to venture further afield.
